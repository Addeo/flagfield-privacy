Privacy Policy — Flagfield

Last updated: May 19, 2026

App: Flagfield (com.flagfield.learn)

Contact: supp0rt.serg@yandex.com

1. Overview

Flagfield (“we”, “the app”) helps you learn countries through interactive games. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Data stored on your device

We do not require an account. The following may be stored locally on your device (for example via Capacitor Preferences / browser storage):

  • Learning progress: countries practiced, correct/incorrect attempts, game mode, whether search was used, timestamps.
  • Settings: UI language, color palette, typography, optional display name.
  • Usage limits: number of completed free game sessions.
  • Daily goal: progress toward your daily target.

You can delete this data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage in system settings.

2.2 Data we do not collect

  • We do not run third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the current app version.
  • We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone data for Flagfield gameplay.

2.3 Purchases and subscriptions

When you buy Premium:

  • Apple App Store or Google Play process payment and may collect data under their policies.
  • RevenueCat (when configured) receives purchase and subscription status to unlock Premium. See RevenueCat Privacy.

2.4 Network content (third-party services)

When you use the app, your device may request resources from:

ServicePurpose
FlagCDN (flagcdn.com)Country flag images (by ISO country code)
CARTO / OpenStreetMapMap tiles for Map Quest (flat political map)
Natural Earth (bundled GeoJSON)Country boundaries on globe and map
Optional country scene videosIf you or we add files under assets/country-scenes/

These providers may see your IP address and technical request data as part of normal web traffic. We do not control their logging practices.

2.5 Country Portrait (long-press on flag)

If you long-press a flag, the app may show a full-screen cultural scene. Video or poster files are loaded from the app bundle or paths defined in the app; no extra account is required.

3. How we use data

  • Run Globe Quest, Map Quest, Flag Quiz, and show your progress.
  • Enforce free-tier session limits and Premium access.
  • Remember your preferences (language, theme).
  • Improve stability when you contact support.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Children

Flagfield is suitable for general audiences including children learning geography. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child provided personal data through support email, contact us and we will delete it.

5. Retention and deletion

Progress and settings remain on your device until you remove them. Subscription records are retained by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat according to their policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on your region (for example GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Email supp0rt.serg@yandex.com. For store purchases and cancellations, use your Apple ID or Google Play subscription settings.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the app. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. International transfers

If you use the app outside your home country, local data may remain on your device while purchase data may be processed by Apple, Google, or RevenueCat in other countries.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
